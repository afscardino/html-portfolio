About Me

Come in! Come in! And know me better, man!.

I've always loved that line from A Christmas Carol. It's great to meet you, I'm Anthony. I've recently become an expat! Hence the numerous titles and hats I wear now. I have had the privilege of working from home, solving geospatial problems for about 2 decades now. I do this by making far fewer maps than I used to or would like to. Hence, my progression into development. I'm still making geospatial things, it's just a different medium. Speaking of medium, I started my journey into geospatial through a bit luck. I moved from graphic design to geography, inspired by my father's work at PennDOT. Shout out to my former Dean, Mr. DiBiase, for helping me see the light.

Personally, I'm married to the best person on earth and the reason I am currently an expat. I am also the service animal to the cutest black cat. He was rescued from a hoarding situation and is now blind. The rest of my family is great too; my brother and sister are far smarter than me. My hobbies have always included making art by any means possible. Right now, I'm most interested in linocut printing. I'm also staggeringly mediocre at hockey. I did a neat little python project with my fitness statistics, you can check it out here. Otherwise, you can catch me on the weekends on a hike or watching soccer (a-hem) I mean football.
